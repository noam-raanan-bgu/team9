About Us

Welcome to DoggyDates!
Doggy dates was founded in 2022 and is designed to be an online social network for dog owners to share a social life all around the country.
The company's activities focus on understanding the dog owners natural need to create shared social frameworks and develop an
active community life for their dogs based on matching data between them.
We bring our community members a fresh, fun and enjoyable spirit.
On Doggy dates we ensure you won't have a single boring moment ....
We are committed to discretion, and assure our surfers that we will not pass on their personal
details to other members of the site, and that their personal details will be passed on by them only.
The site system provides answers to questions and strives to address any issue as quickly as possible.
We continue to grow and evolve - and think 24/7 about ways to make Doggy dates a more successful, satisfying, exciting and fun site for you.
